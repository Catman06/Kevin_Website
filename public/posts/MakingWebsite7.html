After making the posts transition, I wanted to make the post previews do the same. I had to use a TransitionGroup component as the post previews are a v-for directive. TransitionGroups are used for applying transitions to all members of a v-for so I couldn't just move the whole group at once, but the the only real difference in implementation between the previews and the post was how far the previews needed to be set to move. Due to the previews all moving to the same point, rather than staying in position relative to one another, the previews were set to leave the screen just enough to hide them. If the previews were set to leave to a point too far offscreen, the previews would move too fast, if the point is too close, the preview would disappear onscreen.
